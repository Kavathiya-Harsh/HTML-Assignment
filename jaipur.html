Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan in India, is fondly known as the "Pink City" due to the distinct pink hue of its historic buildings. Founded in 1727 by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is famous for its royal heritage, majestic forts, and vibrant markets.

The city is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. Architectural wonders like the Hawa Mahal, City Palace, and Amber Fort draw millions of tourists every year. Jaipur is also part of the famed "Golden Triangle" tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra.

In addition to its rich history, Jaipur is known for its colorful festivals, traditional handicrafts, and delicious Rajasthani cuisine. Whether you're strolling through the old city's bustling bazaars or enjoying a sunset from Nahargarh Fort, Jaipur offers a unique cultural experience.
