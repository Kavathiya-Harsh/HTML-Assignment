Delhi

Delhi, the capital of India, is a vibrant city that blends ancient history with modern culture.
It's home to stunning monuments like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate. Each corner of the city tells a story of dynasties, empires, and revolutions.

The city is known for its bustling markets, delicious street food, and spiritual centers.
Whether you're shopping in Chandni Chowk or visiting the peaceful Lotus Temple, Delhi offers a unique mix of chaos and calm.

I loved exploring the rich cultural diversity in Delhi.
From traditional festivals to contemporary art galleries, the city is a hub of creativity, tradition, and progress.
The energy of Delhi is truly unmatched.
