Mumbai

Mumbai, also known as “The City of Dreams”, is India's financial and entertainment capital.
It's a city that never sleeps — fast-paced, energetic, and full of life. From skyscrapers to slums, Mumbai is a city of contrasts.

One of the must-visit places in Mumbai is the Gateway of India, a historic monument by the sea.
You can also take a boat ride to the ancient Elephanta Caves or relax at the famous Marine Drive, especially at sunset.

What I love most about Mumbai is its spirit — no matter the challenges, the people are full of hope and determination.
The local street food, especially vada pav and pani puri, is simply delicious. Mumbai truly captures the heart of anyone who visits.
